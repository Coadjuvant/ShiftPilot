ShiftPilot Brand Guide (v1)
================================

Primary Marks
-------------
- Dark Mode Icon: purple/pink gradient circle with black upward arrow.
- Light Mode Icon: blue/cyan gradient circle with white upward arrow.

Layouts
-------
- Icon-only (circle + arrow)
- Inline logo: icon + 'ShiftPilot' wordmark
- Stacked logo: icon above 'ShiftPilot'
- Header variants for dark and light backgrounds.

Colors (approximate)
--------------------
- Dark gradient: #FF40FF to #7D3CFF (adjust to taste)
- Light gradient: #7A80FF to #4FD7FF (adjust to taste)
- Dark arrow: near-black / very dark navy (e.g. #05070D)
- Light arrow: #FFFFFF
- Dark background: #050714
- Light background: #F5F7FC

Typography
----------
- Family: Any clean sans-serif (e.g. Inter, DejaVu Sans, SF Pro)
- Weight: Bold or SemiBold for the wordmark 'ShiftPilot'.

Usage Notes
-----------
- Use the purple icon / white wordmark on dark UIs.
- Use the blue icon / black wordmark on light UIs.
- Maintain clearspace around the icon equal to 25% of its diameter.
- Do not stretch, skew, recolor, or add shadows without reason.
